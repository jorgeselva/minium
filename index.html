Why Minium?

At Minium we believe technology should be simple and mindful. We realized that we were constantly distracted and feeling over-connected by our smartphones. To solve this, we decided to build a new phone.

Minium is the first modern minimalist phone, a phone for people that want a phone and nothing more.

Minium features a crisp e-paper display and lasts up to a month on a single charge. The feature set is minimal, the design is beautiful and the user experience is as delightful as on any modern device.

Benefits of Minium phone

Focus

Minium helps you disconnect and reduce mental clutter by removing unecessary distractions

Unplug

A battery efficient, e-paper display keeps you going for weeks, not days on a single charge

Relax

We fundamentally believe in transparency and give you back full ownership over your data
